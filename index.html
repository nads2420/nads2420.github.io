スタジオジブリ Movie List A site I made using a Ghibli API. Bug Hunt A game about catching bugs with your mouse. You have 40 seconds at the start of the game to find bugs which adds seconds to the timer. I created this game for my final project for an Intro to Web Tech class. The game was made with PIXIJs and JavaScript.In the game, I made all of the sprites, and got the audio from royalty free audio libraries. I also practiced utilizing ES6 classes in my code.

Detective Office My final project for 3D Animation and Asset Production. I worked in a group of 4 as the team lead to create a unity scene modeled with MAYA. The theme of the scene is a noir-esque detective's office. I created the greybox in unity and modeled the floors,walls, desk, bookshelf,door, and a few other models in the scene. I was also tasked with putting all the models in the scene and handling the lighting. I created models using techniques learned in class, such as, uv unwrapping, texturing, skinning, and rigging.
